# Your Zawly Dashboard: What You Need to Know

The **dashboard** is the first page you see after signing in. It's your family's command centre — giving you a quick overview of what is happening today and how your family's budget looks, all in one place.

## Why the Dashboard Matters

Most families check their **dashboard** daily because it answers the three big questions:
1. What's happening **today**?
2. How are we doing with **money** this month?
3. **Who's** in our family workspace?

No hunting through different pages — it's all there from the moment you log in.

## Today's Events Section

The top section shows all events scheduled for **today**:

### What You'll See
- **Event titles** with times (e.g., "School Pickup 3:15 PM")
- **Who added them** (helpful in larger families to know who committed to what)
- **Any notes** attached to the event (like "Bring swimming kit")

### Using Today's Events
- Click on an event to view the full details
- Make changes directly if things have shifted
- Quickly see if the day is packed or has breathing room

### For Families with Kids
Encourage children to check the dashboard each morning:
- "What's happening today?"
- "Who's picking me up?"
- "Do I need to bring anything?"

This builds independence and reduces the "What's happening today?" questions.

## Monthly Income and Expenses Summary

The dashboard summarises your family's finances for the **current month**:

### The Three Numbers
| Metric | What It Means |
|--------|----------------|
| **Income** | Total money received this month (salary, benefits, side work) |
| **Expenses** | Total money spent this month (all categories combined) |
| **Left** | What remains (income minus expenses) |

### Why This Matters
- **Green "Left" number** = you're living within your means
- **Red "Left" number** = you've spent more than came in
- **Tracking this daily** helps you adjust spending before the month ends

### Switching Months
The dashboard defaults to the current month, but you can:
- Look back at past months to see spending patterns
- Check future months (once income/expenses are added)
- Understand seasonal spending (December is always more expensive!)

## Family Members Section

See who's in your **family workspace**:

### What's Displayed
- Each member's name
- Their role: **Admin** (can invite/remove members) or **Member** (can view and add)
- Quick access to family settings (if you're an admin)

### Managing Family Members
From the dashboard, admins can:
- **Invite new members** using the "Invite Member" button
- **Change roles** (promote a member to admin)
- **Remove members** if someone leaves the household

### For Larger Families
In families with 5+ members, the dashboard helps you:
- Quickly see who's part of the household
- Identify if someone hasn't logged in recently
- Keep track of who has what permissions

## Recent Transactions

The dashboard shows your **most recent income and expense entries**:

### Why Show Recent Transactions?
- You don't have to navigate to the **Expenses** page to remember what was recorded
- Quick verification: "Yes, I did record that £42 grocery shop"
- Spot mistakes quickly: "That should have been £24, not £42!"

### Viewing Transaction Details
Click on any recent transaction to:
- See the full details (amount, category, date, notes)
- Edit it if something was wrong
- Delete it if it was a mistake

## Getting Around from the Dashboard

The dashboard is your launch pad for the rest of Zawly:

### Quick Navigation
- **Calendar** — view or add events for any day
- **Expenses** — record income or spending, manage categories
- **Settings** — manage your account, change password, update profile
- **Family** — invite members, change roles, leave family

### Keyboard Shortcuts (Mental Ones!)
- Check dashboard **first thing** in the morning
- Review it **Sunday evening** to prep for the week
- Glance at it **after recording an expense** to see the updated totals

## Common Dashboard Questions

**"The 'Left' number looks wrong"**
- Check you've recorded all income for the month
- Make sure recurring expenses are set with the right period
- Remember: it's income minus expenses, not your bank balance

**"I can't see events from last month"**
- The dashboard only shows *today's* events
- Use the Calendar page to look at past or future months
- The dashboard is deliberately focused on *today*

**"Recent transactions aren't showing"**
- Only the most recent few appear on the dashboard
- Go to Expenses page to see the full list
- Make sure you actually recorded them!

**"Can I customise what's on the dashboard?"**
- Currently, the dashboard shows a fixed set of information
- Customisable widgets are being considered for future updates
- The current layout covers what 95% of families need daily

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I change the order of information on the dashboard?**
A: The current layout is fixed, designed to show the most important information first (today's events, then money). Custom layouts may be added in a future update.

**Q: Why doesn't the dashboard show tomorrow's events?**
A: It's focused on *today* to avoid overwhelming users. For tomorrow and beyond, use the Calendar page.

**Q: The income and expense numbers seem wrong — what should I check?**
A: Verify that all transactions have the correct date and period (one-off, weekly, monthly, etc.). Recurring transactions need the right period to display correctly in monthly summaries.

**Q: Can I see a different month's summary on the dashboard?**
A: The dashboard always shows the current month. To view past or future months, go to the Expenses page and use the month selector.

**Q: Is the dashboard updated in real-time?**
A: Yes. When any family member adds an event or records an expense, the dashboard updates immediately for everyone.
