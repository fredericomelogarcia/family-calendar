# Weekly Family Meetings Made Simple

A quick weekly check-in keeps everyone on the same page. Zawly's dashboard makes it easy to review the week gone by and prepare for the one ahead.

## Why Weekly Meetings Help

Families that meet briefly each week:
- Avoid last-minute surprises
- Spot clashes before they happen
- Feel more like a team and less like ships passing in the night

## Using the Dashboard for Your Meeting

Gather around the **Dashboard** (everyone can sign in on their own device, or use one screen):

### 1. Review This Week's Events
The dashboard shows today's events. Scroll through recent days to see:
- What appointments happened
- Which activities were a hit
- What times worked well (and what didn't)

### 2. Check the Money
Look at the **Income vs Expenses** summary:
- Did we overspend in any category?
- Are there one-off expenses coming next week?
- What is left in the monthly budget?

### 3. Plan Ahead
Click through to **Calendar** and **Expenses** to add:
- Next week's school runs and work shifts
- Upcoming birthday parties or social events
- Bills and regular payments due next week

## Involving Everyone

Give each family member a role:
- One person checks the calendar for next week
- Another reviews the expense categories
- Kids can add their own upcoming events

## Making It a Habit

Set a **Weekly** recurring event called "Family Meeting — Sunday 6 PM" on the calendar. After a few weeks, it becomes part of your routine.

The dashboard gives you everything you need in one place — no spreadsheets, no long email threads, just a clear view of family life.
